AIM OF THE HACKATHON
0xStackTooDeep is a 3 days long hackathon organized By BlocSoc(Sponsored Py GWEI & WEI) with the goal of inspiring and fostering a community of Builders promoting technological advancements in the Web3 space with a special focus on Web3 security.
